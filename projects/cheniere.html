Client

Cheniere Energy

Cheniere Energy is an international leading producer of liquefied natural gas (LNG) to provide clean energy to its customers. While undergoing a rebrand to represent its new values, Cheniere needed a secure intranet to provide an evolving library of brand assets and company updates to its employees and partners. The Cheniere Brand Portal is a user-driven designed CMS so all users can easily maneuver in the front-end and back-end. The Cheniere Brand Portal allows users to: upload and download imagery, create collections of assets to share internally or externally, get updates through the company calendar, and view all company information under one umbrella.

Under the guidance of the Creative Director and UX Strategist, we created a fully functional brand CMS. I continued building out the Visual Design System based on the brand design from the Creative Director.
